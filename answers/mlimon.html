The biggest impact of Latino culture is how it expands and connects the Austin lived experience. It is an incredibly diverse cultural expression influenced by fifth-generation Tejanos, global professionals from all over the Americas, recent immigrants striving for the American Dream and everyone in between. It can allow us to truly see each other, to celebrate our differences and to appreciate our similarities.

It's not just breakfast tacos, queso, margaritas, mariachis and ballet folklorico. It's a way of life that has great depth and breadth. Latino culture in Austin has existed for hundreds of years and is constantly evolving while anchored to heart and soul.

The impact sits in our classrooms every day. Austin ISD's student body is 60 percent Latino, and many of these young people are bilingual, bicultural digital natives. Their unique perspective will highly influence a rapidly changing Austin. However, this is only the case if young people have access to positive cultural experiences in all forms.
